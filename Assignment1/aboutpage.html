The Author

Maruyama Hiroshi 丸山浩

Hiroshi Maruyama (丸山浩 Maruyama Hiroshi) is a Japanese illustrator and a character designer. He is well known as an Ultraman designer in early Heisei era.

Intro

Since its debut in 1966, the "Cosmic Hero Ultraman Series" has accompanied and moved generations of people. In its 55-year history, a large number of unique Ultraman and monster characters have emerged in the series and it's all thanks to the artists behind the creation of "Giant of Light". The entire "Cosmic Hero Ultraman Series" can be divided into Showa series, Heisei series, Reiwa series according to the Japanese era. Heng Narita, Hiroshi Maruyama, and Masahiro Goto are the representative designers of these three periods. They all use their own artistic styles to interpret the different Ultra Heroes in their own eyes.If Narita Heng was the first person to see the "Giant of Light", then Maruyama Hiroshi was the successor who passed the touch of light to the world again.

Early Life

Hiroshi Maruyama (born March 21, 1962 in Miyazaki, Japan) graduated from Tokyo Design University, and then worked for the Disney Company in Japan for four years, responsible for the design and art work of the merchandise department. In 1992, he joined Tsuburaya Productions Co., Ltd. and initially worked as an art design for events, and was also responsible for the maintenance of some monster holsters and props.
