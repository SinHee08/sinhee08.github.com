Biography

Having been petrified millions of years in the past, Ultraman Tiga was reawakened when he bonded with Daigo Madoka, giving the GUTS pilot Daigo his powers of Light. The first Ultraman to reawaken in his unique dimension, Tiga utilizes type changes that give him different abilities while protecting Earth from Kaiju threats.

A new Ultra who appeared years after the departure of Ultraman Tiga, Dyna’s power was crucial in the struggle against the invasion of the shapeshifting Sphere. Possessing type change abilities like Tiga, Dyna’s power is a mix of the Ultra-Ancient past, and the unknown future waiting for humanity out among the stars.

Ultraman Gaia is the will of the Earth itself made manifest, striking with incredible power, tempered with the kindness of the bountiful world on which we live. His Photon Edge is more than enough to handle anything the Radical Destruction Bringer can send towards Earth, but when the threat becomes extreme, he can count on Ultraman Agul for help.
